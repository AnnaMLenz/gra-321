About Charlie's Cafe

Charlie’s Café, one of Sun Devil Dining’s local restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU! Proudly serving Lavazza Italian espresso and featuring fresh European sandwiches and baked goods. Enjoy fresh pastries from their bakery, a hot tea from their extensive collection, or just come to chat with a friendly ASU student employee. Each location offers seating for spending time with friends or studying between classes. Come experience the difference! With three convenient locations on campus, there is always a Charlie’s Café, nearby! Charlie’s Café is located at the Hayden Library, Bio Design B Building and the College of Design.
